Child And Old Care

We visited the Child and Old Care Nursing Home in Kalyanpur, Dhaka.From there we got to know many .. things about old age home. There are 67 elderly women aged 50-73, 64 elderly men aged 50-75 and 24 children aged 8-13 under their care. There are 5 male, 6 female staff and 4 doctors to look after them. Those who look after them full-time. This old age home of theirs is completely run by people's donations. And we should donate to support them.
